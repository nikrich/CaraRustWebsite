About

Telling stories through film & theatre - from directing, acting, editing to production, I live for creating. With my strong organisational and logical side combined with my creative and innovative nature, I am well balanced for the fast pace creative industry. I have a degree in theatre - where my love for directing started. From there I entered the exciting film industry, growing my skills everyday. I am currently working in film production at Groundglass in Cape Town, South Africa. I love connecting with creatives, feel free to contact me.
