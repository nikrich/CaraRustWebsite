Who am I?

Cara Rust is a passionate and creative film and theatre director, as well as a video editor, with over a decade of experience. With her extensive background in directing, editing, and motion graphics animation, she has worked on a wide range of local and international projects, from narrative films to promotional videos.

Her latest short film, The Date, has won multiple international awards. She specialises in creating content that reflects real life and inspires audiences to make a difference.
