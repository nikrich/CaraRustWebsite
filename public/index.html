Cara Rust.
Film Director

With a background in theatre directing, Cara has worked in the film industry as part of production and post-production for over 7 years, in Cape Town, South Africa. She began creating her own films, English Made Simple being her debut short film. The film has been selected for over nine international film festivals. Cara is currently working as a creative lead, whilst directing shorts in her own time.
